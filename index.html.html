📏

The 50/30/20 Rule

50% needs, 30% wants, 20% savings. The golden budgeting framework for students!

📱

Track Every Rupee

That ₹20 chai every day = ₹600/month. Tiny habits create massive results over time.

🎯

Set Monthly Goals

₹500/month saved = ₹6,000/year. Use Thing Saver to lock in goals and stay accountable!

🛑

Avoid Impulse Buying

Wait 24 hours before any unplanned purchase. Your future self will thank you.

💳

Beware Credit Cards

Credit card interest = 36–40% annually! Only spend what you already have in your account.

📲

Track UPI Payments

Digital payments are invisible money. Log every transaction right after paying!

🍱

Meal Prep Wins

Cooking on weekends saves 40–60% on food costs. Healthy and budget-friendly!

📚

Share Resources

Split textbook costs with 4 classmates — ₹2000 book becomes ₹500 each!

🐰

Give a Little

Even ₹50/month donated builds your gratitude habit. Generosity always comes back!

🌱

Invest Early

₹1000/month in a mutual fund at 18 = ₹1+ crore by 45. Time is your biggest asset!
